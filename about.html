About PawAdopt

Connecting adopters with rescue dogs across India

Our Mission

At PawAdopt, we believe every dog deserves a loving home. Our mission is to simplify the dog adoption process and connect caring families with rescue dogs in need.

The Problem We're Solving

  • 🏢 Many animal shelters lack online visibility
  • 🔍 Adoption processes are scattered and complex
  • 📍 People don't know where to find adoptable dogs near them
  • 💡 Limited awareness about indie dog adoption

Our Solution

PawAdopt is a centralized platform that brings together adoptable dogs, shelters, and prospective adopters. We make it easy to:

  • ✓ Browse adoptable dogs by city
  • ✓ Connect directly with shelters
  • ✓ Support rescue organizations through donations
  • ✓ Learn about responsible dog adoption

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I adopt a dog through PawAdopt?

+

Simply browse dogs by city, find one you like, click "Start Adoption Process," fill out the application form, and the shelter will contact you within 24-48 hours to discuss next steps.

Are there any adoption fees?

+

Adoption fees vary by shelter. Some shelters charge a nominal fee to help cover medical care and shelter expenses, while others may not charge at all. The shelter will provide details during the adoption process.

Can I adopt a dog from a different city?

+

Yes! You can browse dogs from any city on our platform. However, we recommend starting with dogs in your city for easier coordination and to support local shelters.

What if I'm a first-time dog owner?

+

Many dogs are perfect for first-time owners! Check out our blog section for tips on dog care, training, and preparation. Our shelters also provide guidance to ensure a successful adoption.

How can I help if I can't adopt?

+

You can donate through our platform to support shelter operations, food, medical care, and vaccination. You can also share our platform with others to help more dogs find homes!

Are the dogs vaccinated and healthy?

+

Most dogs on our platform are vaccinated and receive medical checkups. Each dog's profile shows their health status. We encourage all adopters to have a vet checkup before and after adoption.
